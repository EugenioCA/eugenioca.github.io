COBOL Developer

Is a senior independent IT consultant with experience in the full life cycle of the software design process. For more than ten years, he has been involved in the development of software for the financial, government, military and industrial sectors, as a project manager, business analyst, and software developer. After years of working as a software engineer primarily with Python and Java, he decided to move into COBOL to leverage his expertise in legacy systems and meet the demand in critical industries.

In addition, he has more than ten years experience as a chemical engineer in the oil industry, where he has worked in research, planning and operations.

His main assignments were in Africa, South America and Asia. This international experience provided him with the people and mental skills necessary to work in different and challenging environments. In addition, his strong educational background has given him the capacity to quickly adapt to new technical challenges.

He holds a postgraduate level of education in both, Engineering and Economics. He is fluent in English and Spanish. He is a South African and Argentine citizen, at present residing between South Africa and Argentina, and willing to work anywhere in the world.
